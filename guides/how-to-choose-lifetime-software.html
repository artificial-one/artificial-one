How to Choose Lifetime Software Deals

5 Questions to Ask Before Buying

1. Will I use this for 3+ years?

Lifetime deals pay off after 6-24 months. If you'll only use for 3 months, rent instead.

2. Is the company stable?

Check: Founded date, team size, existing customers, reviews. Avoid brand new startups for mission-critical tools.

3. What's the refund policy?

Good: 60 days (AppSumo standard)

Okay: 30 days

Bad: 14 days or less

4. Are features actively being added?

Read reviews. If development stopped 2 years ago, tool may become obsolete.

5. What's my backup plan?

Can you export data? What if company shuts down? Always have exit strategy.

Red Flags

  • Company founded less than 1 year ago
  • No reviews or only 5-star reviews (fake)
  • Missing essential features vs competitors
  • No data export option
  • Founder has history of failed products

Green Flags

  • 100+ reviews averaging 4.5+ stars
  • Regular product updates
  • Active community/forum
  • Responsive support
  • Clear roadmap
