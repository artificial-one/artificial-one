AppSumo Black Friday Strategy

What to Expect

AppSumo typically offers:

  • 20-40% extra discount on select deals
  • Exclusive new product launches
  • Limited-time pricing on popular tools
  • Stack multiple codes for bigger discounts

Best Strategy

Before Black Friday (Now)

  1. Make a wishlist of tools you need
  2. Sign up for AppSumo email list
  3. Set aside budget ($300-1,000)
  4. Research alternatives to compare

During Black Friday

  1. Check deals page first thing Friday morning
  2. Buy essential tools immediately (best deals sell out fast)
  3. Use 60-day refund policy as safety net
  4. Stack codes if available

After Black Friday

  1. Test all tools within 60 days
  2. Refund what doesn't work
  3. Keep winners

Top Tools to Watch For

  • Email marketing (SendFox, MailerLite)
  • Design tools (Pixelied, Glorify)
  • SEO tools (ClickRank, NeuronWriter)
  • Productivity (TidyCal, Notion)
  • Course platforms (Zenler, Graphy)

Budget Recommendations

Starter stack: $200-300

Business stack: $500-1,000

Agency stack: $1,500-3,000
