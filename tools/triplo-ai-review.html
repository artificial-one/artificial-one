What is Triplo AI?

Triplo AI is a desktop AI assistant that works everywhere on your computer. Instead of copying text to ChatGPT, Triplo brings the AI directly into any app you're using—Gmail, Slack, Google Docs, Notion, Twitter, you name it.

Press a keyboard shortcut (Command+J on Mac, Ctrl+J on Windows), and Triplo's AI window appears instantly. Ask it to write, rewrite, translate, summarize, or answer questions—all without leaving your current app.

Key differentiator: Unlike ChatGPT or other AI tools that require switching tabs or copying/pasting, Triplo integrates directly into your workflow. It's like having an AI co-pilot that follows you everywhere.
