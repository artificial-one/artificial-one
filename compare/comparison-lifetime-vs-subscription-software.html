Lifetime vs Subscription Software: The Math

Real Example: Email Marketing

Mailchimp (10K contacts): $100/month = $1,200/year = $6,000 over 5 years

SendFox (5K contacts): $49 once

Savings: $5,951 over 5 years

When Lifetime Wins

  • Tool you'll use for 3+ years
  • Stable feature set (not rapidly changing)
  • No enterprise needs
  • Budget-conscious

When Subscription Wins

  • Mission-critical tools (CRM, accounting)
  • Need latest features constantly
  • Enterprise support required
  • Regulatory compliance needs

The Smart Strategy

Go lifetime for: Email, design, SEO, scheduling, productivity ($500-1,000 once)

Keep subscription for: Critical business tools ($100-300/month)

Result: Save $5,000-15,000/year while maintaining professional stack
