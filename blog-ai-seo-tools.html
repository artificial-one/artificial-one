Which Should You Choose?

Choose Surfer SEO if:

You want the most comprehensive data, run an agency, or manage multiple client sites. Worth the investment for serious SEO work.

Choose Clearscope if:

You have budget and prioritize ease of use. Perfect for enterprise teams with multiple writers who need a simple workflow.

Choose Frase if:

You're on a budget and want SEO + AI writing combined. Great for solo bloggers and small teams starting with content SEO.

The Bottom Line

For most professional content teams, Surfer SEO offers the best balance of depth and usability. If budget isn't a concern and you want simplicity, Clearscope is excellent. Solo creators on a budget should start with Frase.

All three tools will improve your rankings - the question is whether you need enterprise features or can succeed with a more affordable option.
