How to Use ChatGPT for Business: 15 Practical Use Cases

Real-world applications that save time and money. Includes prompts and examples.

ChatGPT can save businesses 10-20 hours per week when used correctly. Here are 15 proven use cases with real prompts you can copy.

1. Customer Service Responses

Time Saved: 5-8 hours/week

Draft responses to common customer questions in seconds. Maintain brand voice and handle edge cases professionally.

Prompt: "Draft a professional, empathetic response to this customer complaint: [paste complaint]. Our company policy is [policy]. Keep it under 100 words and offer a solution."

2. Meeting Preparation & Agendas

Time Saved: 2-3 hours/week

Generate meeting agendas, prep talking points, and create follow-up action items automatically.

Prompt: "Create a meeting agenda for a 30-minute product roadmap review with [stakeholders]. Include: current status, key decisions needed, and next steps."

3. Email Writing & Optimization

Time Saved: 3-5 hours/week

Write cold outreach, follow-ups, internal communications, and sales emails in your brand voice.

4. Content Marketing Ideas

Time Saved: 2-4 hours/week

Generate blog topics, social posts, video scripts, and email newsletter ideas based on your audience.

5. Data Analysis & Insights

Time Saved: 2-3 hours/week

Analyze survey results, customer feedback, sales data, and identify patterns or trends.

6-15. More Use Cases

  • 6. Code Documentation: Auto-generate README files and code comments
  • 7. Job Descriptions: Write compelling JDs that attract top talent
  • 8. Sales Scripts: Create personalized outreach for different segments
  • 9. Training Materials: Convert docs into onboarding content
  • 10. Legal Summaries: Summarize contracts (review with lawyer!)
  • 11. Competitive Analysis: Research competitors and market trends
  • 12. Product Descriptions: Write SEO-optimized ecommerce copy
  • 13. Brainstorming: Generate creative solutions to business problems
  • 14. Translation: Localize content for international markets
  • 15. Process Documentation: Create SOPs and workflow guides
